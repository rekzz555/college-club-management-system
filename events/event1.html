Delilah - A Tale Of Forgiveness

Event Date: Apr 10, 2025

About the Event

On April 10, 2025, the Department of English enthralled audiences with an original theatrical production titled "Delilah: A Tale of Forgiveness", a bold reinterpretation of Greek mythology conceptualized and performed entirely by undergraduate students. The play traces the poignant journey of the god Apollo as he becomes enamoured by a mortal woman, exploring timeless themes of love, power, and vengeance against a backdrop of divine conflict. Skillfully directed by final-year students from the English and Psychology departments, the production featured evocative set designs and immersive staging that highlighted the script's emotional intensity. Receiving widespread acclaim from faculty and peers alike, the performance was commended by the Financial Administrator for its inventive storytelling and depth, standing as a powerful testament to the department's commitment to fostering original artistic excellence and intellectual growth.
