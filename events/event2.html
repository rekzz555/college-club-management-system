Nrityadarpan 2025: Fiction Reimagined – Stories Through Movements

Event Date: Apr 15, 2025

About the Event

On February 28, 2025, the grand annual dance recital "Nrityadarpan 2025" unfolded as a mesmerizing celebration of dance, storytelling, and imagination centered on the theme "Fiction Reimagined – Stories Through Movements". The event brought legendary fictional characters to life through ten stunning performances by 100 talented dancers, weaving new narratives into well-loved tales across two spectacular shows. The morning session was graced by a distinguished Bharathanatyam dancer as the Chief Guest alongside leadership and management, all of whom joined faculty and attendees in meting out resounding applause for the performers' artistic excellence. The production stood as a testament to the dancers' rigorous training and creative ingenuity, transforming a traditional showcase into a vivid re-imagination of storytelling through movement. As the academy continues to nurture young artists, the event remains a shining beacon of cultural expression and original artistic achievement.
