Talestra: An Intercollegiate Literary Fest

Event Date: Apr 15, 2025

About the Event

On February 24, 2025, the Writers' Association hosted "Talestra 2025", an inaugural intercollegiate fest featuring 93 participants from nine colleges. The event opened with a keynote on the power of writing and featured five creative competitions, including Treasure Hunt and Flip the Script. Emphasizing the value of human imagination over artificial intelligence, the fest concluded by celebrating literary talent and announcing the overall champions and runners-up, marking a successful debut for the collaborative platform.
